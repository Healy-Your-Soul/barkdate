Introduction

Welcome to Bark! ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo
  • Dog Profile: Your dog's name, breed, age, photos, and bio
  • Location Data: Your location to find nearby dogs and parks (only when you grant permission)
  • Messages: Communications with other users
  • Photos: Images you upload of your dog

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To connect you with other dog owners nearby
  • To facilitate playdates and events
  • To send notifications about messages and friend requests
  • To ensure the safety and security of our community

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your public profile (dog info, photos) is visible to other users
  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate our app (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase and Firebase for data storage and authentication, which comply with modern security standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

Location Data

We only access your location when you explicitly grant permission. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time. Location is used to:

  • Find dogs and dog-friendly places near you
  • Show your approximate location to potential playdate matches

Children's Privacy

Bark! is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
