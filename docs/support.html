Q: How do I create a profile for my dog?
A: After signing up, tap the Profile tab and select "Add Dog". Fill in your dog's name, breed, age, and upload some adorable photos!
Q: How do I find dogs near me?
A: Open the Map tab to see dog-friendly places and dogs checked in nearby. Make sure you've enabled location permissions in your device settings.
Q: How do I schedule a playdate?
A: Find a dog you'd like to meet, tap their profile, and send them a "Bark" (friend request). Once accepted, you can message each other and schedule a playdate in the Playdates tab.
Q: Why can't I see the map?
A: Make sure location services are enabled for Bark! in your device settings. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services ‚Üí Bark! ‚Üí "While Using the App".
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.
Q: Is Bark! free to use?
A: Yes! Bark! is completely free to download and use.
